About

This site was made to showcase both my love for palms and my beginner skills as a front end web developer. Since a young age I have been interested in palm trees and how they relate to climate. I decided to focus on the state of florida because it is the state with the most variety of palms and it is a state I am familiar with. The USDA hardiness data is a usuable online formate is not free so I had to create my own usable data using the USDA hardiness map.
